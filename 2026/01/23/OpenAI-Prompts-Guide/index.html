1. 文档说明

OpenAI Academy发布了超过300个专业设计的Prompts，涵盖20多个职业角色。这些Prompt Packs是经过精心策划的高质量提示，专门针对不同工作场景优化，帮助用户在不同职业角色中充分发挥ChatGPT的潜力。

📚 使用指南 (Usage Guide)

如何使用这些Prompts

How to Use These Prompts

  1. 选择合适的Prompt: 根据您的具体需求选择最相关的Prompt。
  2. 替换占位符: 将方括号 [...] 中的内容替换为您的具体信息。
  3. 添加上下文: 根据需要添加更多背景信息以获得更好的结果。
  4. 迭代优化: 如果结果不理想，可以调整Prompt或添加更多细节。

最佳实践

Best Practices

  • 具体化: 越具体的Prompt，结果越精确。
  • 提供背景: 包含相关背景信息帮助ChatGPT理解您的需求。
  • 明确格式: 指定您期望的输出格式（表格、列表、段落等）。
  • 迭代改进: 根据初始结果调整和优化您的Prompt。

2. 销售与客户成功

Sales & Customer Success

2.1 拓展与沟通 (Outreach & Communication)

起草个性化冷邮件

Draft personalized cold outreach email

“Draft a personalized cold outreach email for [prospect name] at [company name]. Focus on their recent [news/achievement/challenge] and explain how [our product] can help them achieve [specific goal]. Keep it under 200 words and include a clear call to action.”

“为[公司名称]的[潜在客户姓名]起草一封个性化的冷开场邮件。聚焦他们最近的[新闻/成就/挑战]，并解释[我们的产品]如何帮助他们实现[具体目标]。保持200字以内，并包含明确的行动号召。”

重新撰写演示跟进邮件

Rework demo follow-up email

“Review this demo follow-up email and rewrite it to be more compelling. Highlight the key value propositions discussed during the demo, address any objections raised, and create urgency for the next steps. Make it concise but impactful.”

“审阅这封演示跟进邮件，并重写使其更有说服力。强调演示中讨论的关键价值主张，解决提出的任何异议，并为下一步创造紧迫感。使其简洁但有影响力。”

起草续约pitch

Draft a renewal pitch

“Create a renewal pitch for [customer name] who has been with us for [duration]. Include their key achievements using our product, ROI metrics if available, and propose a [new term/updated plan] that addresses their evolving needs. Focus on partnership value rather than just transaction.”

“为与我们合作了[时长]的[客户名称]创建一个续约pitch。包括他们使用我们产品取得的主要成就、如果有ROI指标，以及提议一个满足他们不断变化需求的[新期限/更新方案]。专注于合作价值而不仅仅是交易。”

创建代表活动摘要

Create a summary of rep activity

“Analyze this week’s sales representative activity data and create a summary highlighting: top performers, key challenges encountered, deals progressed to next stage, and recommendations for the upcoming week. Format as a concise briefing document.”

“分析本周销售代表的活动数据，并创建摘要突出显示：表现最佳者、遇到的主要挑战、推进到下一阶段的交易，以及下周的建议。格式为简明的简报文件。”

起草高管管道状态更新

Draft an exec update on pipeline status

“Create an executive-level pipeline status update covering: overall pipeline value and health, top 5 opportunities by value and probability, key risks and mitigation strategies, and forecast for [time period]. Use clear metrics and visual aids where helpful.”

“创建高管级别的管道状态更新，包括：整体管道价值和健康状况、按价值和概率排列的前5个机会、关键风险和缓解策略，以及[时间段]的预测。使用清晰的指标和在有帮助的地方使用视觉辅助工具。”

起草销售会议邀请

Draft sales meeting invite

“Draft a professional meeting invite for a sales kickoff meeting. Include agenda items, key objectives, required preparation, and any materials that need to be reviewed beforehand.”

“为销售启动会议起草专业的会议邀请。包括议程项目、关键目标、所需准备，以及任何需要事先审查的材料。”

创建销售跟进计划

Create a sales follow-up plan

“Create a structured follow-up plan for a prospect after their first demo. Include timing for each touchpoint, communication channels to use, and key messages to convey at each stage.”

“为潜在客户在第一次演示后创建一个结构化的跟进计划。包括每个接触点的时机、使用沟通渠道，以及每个阶段传达的关键信息。”

2.2 销售策略与规划 (Sales Strategy & Planning)

战略账户规划

Strategic account planning

“Develop a strategic account plan for [key account] including: current relationship status, key stakeholders and their influence maps, competitive positioning, growth opportunities, and a 90-day action plan with milestones.”

“为[重点客户]制定战略账户计划，包括：当前关系状态、关键利益相关者及其影响图、竞争定位、增长机会，以及包含里程碑的90天行动计划。”

销售演示开发

Sales presentation development

“Assist me in creating a compelling sales presentation for [product/service], aimed at [target audience]. Include key value propositions, customer success stories, competitive advantages, and a clear call to action.”

“帮助我为[产品/服务]创建一个有说服力的销售演示，面向[目标受众]。包括关键价值主张、客户成功案例、竞争优势，以及明确的行动号召。”

销售目标设定

Sales goal setting

“Help me set realistic sales goals for [time period] based on historical performance, market conditions, and team capacity. Include both revenue targets and activity metrics like calls, meetings, and demos.”

“帮助我基于历史绩效、市场条件和团队能力为[时间段]设定现实的销售目标。包括收入目标和活动指标，如电话、会议和演示。”

销售团队激励设计

Sales team motivation design

“Design a motivation and recognition program for the sales team that includes: performance incentives, recognition milestones, team-building activities, and ways to celebrate wins.”

“为销售团队设计激励和认可计划，包括：绩效激励、认可里程碑、团队建设活动，以及庆祝胜利的方式。”

销售流程优化

Sales process optimization

“Review our current sales process and identify bottlenecks, inefficiencies, or gaps. Propose improvements that could shorten sales cycles, increase win rates, or improve forecast accuracy.”

“审查我们当前的销售流程，识别瓶颈、低效或差距。提出可以缩短销售周期、提高胜率或改善预测准确性的改进建议。”

2.3 竞争情报与赋能 (Competitive Intelligence & Enablement)

竞品对比分析

Competitive battlecard creation

“Create a competitive battlecard for [competitor name] focusing on their strengths, weaknesses, key differentiators, and recommended positioning against them. Include specific talking points for common objections.”

“为[竞品名称]创建一个竞争对比卡，重点关注他们的优势、劣势、关键差异点，以及针对他们的推荐定位。包括针对常见异议的具体谈话要点。”

市场机会评估

Market opportunity assessment

“Analyze the current market landscape for [product category] and identify key opportunities for growth. Include market size estimates, emerging trends, customer pain points not well addressed by current solutions, and strategic recommendations.”

“分析[产品类别]当前的市场格局，确定增长的关键机会。包括市场规模估计、新兴趋势、当前解决方案未能很好解决的客户痛点，以及战略建议。”

销售赋能培训大纲

Sales enablement training outline

“Create a training outline for new sales hires covering: product knowledge, sales methodologies, objection handling, and territory management. Include learning objectives and assessment methods.”

“为新销售员工创建培训大纲，涵盖：产品知识、销售方法论、异议处理和区域管理。包括学习目标和评估方法。”

客户洞察报告

Customer insights report

“Compile a customer insights report based on recent sales interactions, feedback surveys, and support tickets. Identify patterns in customer needs, pain points, and buying behavior.”

“基于最近的销售互动、反馈调查和支持票据编译客户洞察报告。识别客户需求、痛点和购买行为的模式。”

行业趋势分析

Industry trend analysis

“Research and analyze current trends in [industry] that could impact our sales strategy. Include technology advancements, regulatory changes, competitor moves, and customer behavior shifts.”

“研究和分析[行业]中可能影响我们销售策略的当前趋势。包括技术进步、监管变化、竞争对手举措和客户行为转变。”

2.4 数据分析与绩效洞察 (Data Analysis & Performance Insights)

销售绩效分析

Sales performance analysis

“Analyze sales team performance data for [time period] and identify: top performers and their best practices, underperformers and coaching opportunities, team-wide trends, and actionable recommendations.”

“分析[时间段]销售团队绩效数据，识别：表现最佳者和他们的最佳实践、表现不佳者和辅导机会、团队范围趋势，以及可操作的建议。”

销售预测建模

Sales forecasting model

“Build a sales forecast for [time period] using historical data, current pipeline, and market conditions. Include best case, worst case, and most likely scenarios with assumptions clearly stated.”

“使用历史数据、当前管道和市场条件为[时间段]构建销售预测。包括最佳情况、最坏情况和最可能情况，并清楚说明假设。”

客户终身价值计算

Customer lifetime value calculation

“Calculate customer lifetime value (CLV) for different customer segments based on historical purchase data, retention rates, and profit margins. Provide insights on which segments to prioritize.”

“基于历史购买数据、保留率和利润率计算不同客户群的客户终身价值（CLV）。提供关于优先考虑哪些群的洞察。”

销售漏斗分析

Sales funnel analysis

“Analyze our sales funnel conversion rates at each stage and identify where prospects are dropping off. Provide recommendations to improve conversion rates and reduce sales cycle length.”

“分析我们销售漏斗在每个阶段的转化率，并识别潜在客户在哪里流失。提供改善转化率和缩短销售周期的建议。”

销售仪表板设计

Sales dashboard design

“Design a sales dashboard that includes key metrics like revenue, pipeline value, win rates, average deal size, and sales velocity. Suggest visual representations and alerts for important thresholds.”

“设计一个销售仪表板，包括关键指标如收入、管道价值、胜率、平均交易规模和销售速度。建议视觉表示和重要阈值的警报。”

2.5 视觉效果与销售辅助材料 (Visuals & Sales Collateral)

销售演示幻灯片大纲

Sales presentation slide deck outline

“Create an outline for a sales presentation deck on [topic] with recommended slide structure, key content for each slide, and visual suggestions to make it engaging.”

“为[主题]创建一个销售演示幻灯片大纲，具有推荐的幻灯片结构、每个幻灯片的关键内容，以及使其引人入胜的视觉建议。”

产品对比图表创建

Product comparison chart creation

“Create a visual comparison chart showing how our product stacks up against [competitor products]. Include features, pricing, and key benefits in an easy-to-read format.”

“创建一个视觉对比图表，显示我们的产品如何与[竞争产品]相媲美。以易读格式包括功能、定价和关键优势。”

销售案例研究开发

Sales case study development

“Develop a compelling customer success story/case study including: customer background, challenges faced, solution implemented, results achieved, and quotes from key stakeholders.”

“开发一个引人注目的客户成功故事/案例研究，包括：客户背景、面临的挑战、实施的解决方案、取得的成果，以及关键利益相关者的引用。”

销售邮件模板设计

Sales email template design

“Design a series of email templates for different stages of the sales process: initial outreach, follow-up after demo, proposal delivery, and negotiation. Include personalization tips and subject line suggestions.”

“为销售流程的不同阶段设计一系列邮件模板：初始接触、演示后跟进、提案交付和谈判。包括个性化提示和主题行建议。”

ROI计算器工具

ROI calculator tool

“Design an ROI calculator tool that prospects can use to estimate the value of implementing our solution. Include input fields for relevant metrics and clear output showing potential savings or gains.”

“设计一个潜在客户可以使用的ROI计算器工具，来估计实施我们解决方案的价值。包括相关指标的输入字段和显示潜在节省或收益的清晰输出。”

2.6 客户成功 (Customer Success)

创建入职计划模板

Create onboarding plan template

“Create a reusable onboarding plan template for [type of customer]. Reference typical timelines, milestones, and stakeholder alignment needs. Format as a week-by-week table with task owners and goals.”

“为[客户类型]创建可重用的入职计划模板。参考典型时间表、里程碑和利益相关者协调需求。格式为每周表格，包含任务负责人和目标。”

总结入职反馈

Summarize onboarding feedback

“Summarize onboarding feedback from our last 10 customers in [segment]. Use these shared notes and survey answers. Output a short paragraph per theme: wins, blockers, suggestions.”

“总结我们最近[细分]中10个客户的入职反馈。使用这些共享笔记和调查答案。每个主题输出简短段落：成功点、障碍、建议。”

识别高接触入职的最佳实践

Identify best practices for high-touch onboarding

“Research how leading B2B companies structure high-touch onboarding journeys. Focus on companies with $1M+ ACV and hybrid onboarding models. Include sources and structure the output as a bulleted summary of key tactics with references.”

“研究领先的B2B公司如何构建高接触入职旅程。专注于具有100万美元以上ACV和混合入职模式的公司。包括来源并将输出结构化为关键策略的带引用要点总结。”

建议主动行动手册

Suggest proactive playbooks

“Recommend 3 proactive outreach playbooks for at-risk customers in [industry/segment]. Use trends from recent churn, feature inactivity, and low engagement. Output should include: goal, trigger, CTA, and timing.”

“为[行业/细分]中面临风险的客户推荐3个主动外展行动手册。使用最近的流失、功能不活跃和低参与度的趋势。输出应包括：目标、触发器、CTA和时机。”

头脑风暴保留激励措施

Brainstorm retention incentives

“Suggest creative retention strategies for accounts likely to downgrade in [industry]. Use trends in usage and renewal hesitations we’ve seen. Output 5 tested and 5 novel ideas with pros/cons.”

“为[行业]中可能降级的账户建议创意保留策略。使用我们看到的用量和续约犹豫趋势。输出5个经过测试和5个新颖的想法，并附有优缺点。”

2.7 竞争与基准研究 (Competitive & Benchmark Research)

基准CS组织结构

Benchmark CS org structure

“Benchmark the CS org structure for companies like ours in [industry, size]. Focus on roles per customer segment and ratio to revenue. Output as a comparison table with notes on headcount ratios.”

“为[行业、规模]类似我们公司的CS组织结构进行基准测试。专注于每个客户群的角色和与收入的比率。输出为比较表格，并附上人员配置比率注释。”

按行业基准成功指标

Benchmark success metrics by industry

“Research top 3 success metrics used for customer health scoring in the [industry] sector. Include CSAT, NRR, usage frequency, or other emerging benchmarks. Output as a table comparing metric, source, and benchmark value with citations.”

“研究[行业]领域用于客户健康评分的3个主要成功指标。包括CSAT、NRR、使用频率或其他新兴基准。输出为表格，比较指标、来源和基准值，并附上引用。”

评估CS工具堆栈

Evaluate CS tooling stacks

“Research typical Customer Success tech stacks for companies in early-stage, growth-stage, and enterprise. Include categories (e.g., CRM, Success Platform, Analytics). Output a comparison chart with examples and usage notes.”

“研究早期、成长期和企业阶段公司的典型客户成功技术堆栈。包括类别（如CRM、成功平台、分析）。输出为比较图表，并附有示例和使用说明。”

竞争支持总结

Competitive enablement summary

“Research how competitors are supporting enterprise customers post-sale in [industry]. Include examples of success resources, team structure, and onboarding formats. Output as a table comparing 3 competitors with pros/cons per tactic.”

“研究竞争对手如何在[行业]中支持企业客户售后。包括成功资源、团队结构和入职格式的示例。输出为比较表格，比较3个竞争对手的每个策略的优缺点。”

创建CS项目竞争对比

Create competitive comparison of CS programs

“Research what customer success programs look like at our top 3 competitors. Focus on onboarding, health tracking, and expansion strategies. Output a comparison matrix.”

“研究我们前3个竞争对手的客户成功项目的样子。专注于入职、健康跟踪和扩展策略。输出为比较矩阵。”

2.8 账户规划与QBR (Account Planning & QBRs)

起草高管邮件更新

Draft executive email update

“Write a weekly update email for [executive stakeholder at customer]. Use these internal notes from this week’s call and usage metrics: [paste here]. Output should be a short, polished email with 3 bullets.”

“为[客户的高管利益相关者]撰写每周更新邮件。使用本周电话的内部笔记和使用指标：[粘贴此处]。输出应为简短的、精致的邮件，包含3个要点。”

起草QBR讨论要点

Draft QBR talking points

“Summarize the top wins, risks, and product usage highlights for [Customer Name] ahead of our QBR. Use their latest health score, usage trends, and support ticket history. Format as a bulleted prep doc for internal review.”

“在为[客户名称]进行QBR之前，总结主要成功点、风险和产品使用亮点。使用他们最新的健康评分、使用趋势和支持工单历史。格式为内部审查的要点准备文档。”

准备续约电话

Prep for renewal call

“Create a renewal call prep checklist for [Customer Name]. Include contract terms, current usage, known risks, and upsell potential. Output as a bulleted checklist.”

“为[客户名称]创建续约电话准备清单。包括合同条款、当前使用情况、已知风险和追加销售潜力。输出为要点清单。”

创建账户计划摘要

Create account plan summary

“Draft a 1-pager account plan for [Customer Name]. Use notes from our last 2 calls + contract info + goals: [paste here]. Output should be formatted as goals, blockers, actions, and renewals.”

“为[客户名称]起草一页账户计划。使用我们最近2次电话的笔记+合同信息+目标：[粘贴此处]。输出应格式化为目标、障碍、行动和续约。”

概述续约风险摘要

Outline renewal risk summary

“Draft a renewal risk summary for [Customer Name] ahead of our internal forecast call. Include their renewal date, usage trend, sentiment, and contract notes. Output should be a paragraph summary + 1-line recommendation.”

“在我们内部预测电话之前，为[客户名称]起草续约风险摘要。包括他们的续约日期、使用趋势、情绪和合同注释。输出应为段落摘要+1行建议。”

2.9 数据分析与洞察 (Data Analysis & Insights)

概述按细分的成功指标

Outline success metrics by segment

“Outline a draft list of success metrics for [segment] customers. Include adoption goals, engagement targets, and renewal benchmarks. Format as a 2-column table: Metric | Definition.”

“为[细分]客户概述成功指标的草案列表。包括采用目标、参与目标和续约基准。格式为2列表格：指标|定义。”

评估CSAT分数分布

Evaluate CSAT score distribution

“Review this CSAT survey data from Q2. Calculate overall average, identify outlier scores, and summarize feedback themes if available. Output as a short summary with key stats and top positive/negative feedback examples.”

“审查Q2的CSAT调查数据。计算整体平均值，识别异常分数，并总结反馈主题（如有）。输出为简短摘要，包含关键统计数据和顶部的正面/负面反馈示例。”

分析支持工单趋势

Analyze support ticket trends

“Examine this export of support tickets from the last quarter. Identify the top 5 recurring issues and provide a short summary of root causes. Output should include a ranked list with issue, frequency, and potential CS actions.”

“检查上季度的支持工单导出。识别前5个重复出现的问题，并提供根本原因的简短摘要。输出应包括排名列表，包含问题、频率和可能的CS行动。”

“我是[临床角色，如：住院医师、重症监护室主治医师、执业护士、医师助理]，正在管理一位[年龄]岁的[性别]患者，因[主要诊断]和[关键复杂问题]入院。创建基于问题的评估和计划，包括：每个活动问题的病理生理学、需追踪或随访的检查、治疗计划（药物、输液、程序、监测）、处置和出院计划。突出[合并症或并发症]如何影响管理，以及什么会触发护理的升级或降级。”

对照循证医学检查

Checking against the evidence

“I am a [clinical role, e.g., cardiologist, pulmonologist, hospitalist] reviewing the care of a [age]-year-old [gender] with [condition] and [key comorbidities]. Summarize the current guideline-based recommendations for: Diagnosis and risk stratification, First-line and second-line therapies, Prevention of complications. Then explain how these guidelines apply to this specific patient given [relevant risk factors or constraints].”

“我是[临床角色，如：心脏病学家、肺科医师、住院医师]，正在回顾一位[年龄]岁的[性别]患者，有[疾病]和[关键合并症]的护理。总结当前基于指南的建议：诊断和风险分层、一线和二线治疗、并发症预防。然后解释鉴于[相关风险因素或限制]，这些指南如何适用于这位特定患者。”

检索和应用临床参考文献

Retrieving and applying clinical references

“I am a [clinical role/specialty, e.g., neurologist, geriatrician, PCP] following a [age]-year-old [gender] patient who had [index neurologic event/condition, e.g., ischemic stroke/TIA/ICH] [timeframe, e.g., 2 years] ago with [degree of residual deficits, e.g., minimal residual deficits / moderate aphasia / mild hemiparesis]. At [his/her/their] most recent visit, [he/she/they] reported [cognitive concern, e.g., memory decline], including [example, e.g., losing phone/missed appointments/repeating questions], and has become [behavior or mood change, e.g., more irritable / apathetic / anxious]. [His/her/their] functional status in daily activities is [mostly preserved / mildly impaired / declining]. Should these symptoms raise suspicion for [condition, e.g., dementia / vascular cognitive impairment / Alzheimer’s disease], or are they more consistent with [normal age-related cognitive changes]? Provide a differential diagnosis and explain why it’s likely or not.”

“我是[临床角色/专科，如：神经科医生、老年科医生、全科医生]，正在随访一位[年龄]岁的[性别]患者，该患者[时间]前有[索引神经事件/疾病，如：缺血性中风/短暂性脑缺血发作/脑出血]，伴有[残留缺陷程度，如：最小残留缺陷/中度失语/轻度偏瘫]。在[他/她/他们]最近的就诊中，[他/她/他们]报告了[认知问题，如：记忆力下降]，包括[例子，如：丢手机/错过预约/重复问题]，并且变得[行为或情绪变化，如：更易怒/冷漠/焦虑]。[他/她/他们]的日常活动功能状态是[大部分保留/轻度受损/下降]。这些症状是否应该引起对[疾病，如：痴呆/血管性认知障碍/阿尔茨海默病]的怀疑，还是更符合[正常年龄相关认知变化]？提供鉴别诊断并解释可能性。”

11.2 临床文档 (Clinical Documentation)

患者就诊文档记录

Documenting the patient encounter

“I am a [clinical role, e.g., pediatrician, family medicine physician, resident] seeing a [age]-year-old [gender] with [chief complaint] and [key symptoms] in the [clinic / ED / hospital]. Write a concise but thorough clinical note including: History of present illness, Relevant past history and medications, Focused physical exam, Assessment with differential, Plan (diagnostics, treatment, and follow-up). Format it the way it would appear in a real chart.”

“我是[临床角色，如：儿科医生、家庭医生、住院医师]，正在看诊一位[年龄]岁的[性别]患者，有[主诉]和[关键症状]，在[诊所/急诊室/医院]。撰写简洁但全面的临床记录，包括：现病史、相关既往史和用药、针对性的体格检查、评估（带鉴别诊断）、计划（诊断、治疗和随访）。按照真实病历的格式撰写。”

安全过渡护理

Safely transitioning care

“I am a [clinical role, e.g., hospital discharge planner, hospitalist, case manager] coordinating care for a [age]-year-old [gender] being discharged after [hospitalization or condition]. Outline the key information that must be communicated to: [receiving provider, e.g., primary care physician], [home health or rehab service], [specialist, if applicable]. Include active problems, medications, pending tests, functional status, and follow-up needs, formatted as a clear handoff summary.”

“我是[临床角色，如：医院出院规划师、住院医师、病例管理员]，正在协调一位[年龄]岁的[性别]患者在[住院或疾病]后的出院护理。概述必须传达给[接收方，如：初级保健医生]、[家庭护理或康复服务]、[专科医生（如适用）]的关键信息。包括活动性问题、药物、待检项目、功能状态和随访需求，格式化为清晰的交接总结。”

使用组织系统指导护理

Using your system to guide care

“Summarize the recommended initial evaluation for [clinical problem, e.g., new cognitive concerns / new weakness / unexplained weight loss] in a [age]-year-old [gender] with [relevant history, e.g., prior ischemic stroke, heart failure, diabetes]. Use our organization’s [approved pathway / local guideline / service-line protocol] if available and link to the relevant section or document. Include key red flags, the focused history and exam (including [collateral sources, meds, or risk factors]), recommended screening tool(s), baseline labs, and when imaging [or specialist referral] is indicated.”

“总结对[年龄]岁[性别]患者，有[相关病史，如：既往缺血性中风、心力衰竭、糖尿病]，针对[临床问题，如：新认知问题/新发无力/无法解释的体重下降]推荐的初步评估。如果有的话，使用我们组织的[批准路径/本地指南/服务线协议]并链接到相关部分或文档。包括关键警示信号、针对性的病史和体检（包括[信息来源、药物或风险因素]）、推荐的筛查工具、基线实验室检查，以及何时需要进行影像学检查[或专科转诊]。”

11.3 患者沟通与指导 (Patient Communication & Counseling)

患者咨询与下一步指导

Counseling the patient and setting next steps

“I am a [clinical role, e.g., endocrinologist, primary care physician, nurse practitioner] counseling a [age]-year-old [gender] with [diagnosis]. Write patient-friendly after-visit instructions that explain: What the condition means, How [medications] should be taken, Key diet, lifestyle, and monitoring recommendations, Red-flag symptoms that should prompt urgent care. Use clear, non-technical language appropriate for a [health-literacy level] patient.”

“我是[临床角色，如：内分泌科医生、初级保健医生、执业护士]，正在为一位[年龄]岁的[性别]患者，有[诊断]，进行咨询。撰写患者友好的就诊后说明，解释：疾病意味着什么、[药物]应如何服用、关键的饮食、生活方式和监测建议、应提示紧急护理的警示症状。使用适合[健康素养水平]患者的清晰、非技术性语言。”

12. 通用工作场景

General Work

12.1 沟通与写作 (Communication & Writing)

撰写专业邮件

Write a professional email

“Write a professional email to [recipient]. The email is about [topic] and should be polite, clear, and concise. Provide a subject line and a short closing.”

“为[收件人]撰写一封专业邮件。邮件关于[主题]，应该礼貌、清晰且简洁。提供主题行和简短的结束语。”

重写以提高清晰度

Rewrite for clarity

“Rewrite the following text so it is easier to understand. The text will be used in a professional setting. Ensure the tone is clear, respectful, and concise. Text: [paste text].”

“重写以下文本，使其更容易理解。文本将在专业环境中使用。确保语气清晰、尊重且简洁。文本：[粘贴文本]。”

调整消息以适应受众

Adapt message for audience

“Reframe this message for [audience type: executives, peers, or customers]. The message was originally written for [context]. Adjust tone, word choice, and style to fit the intended audience. Text: [paste text].”

“为[受众类型：高管、同事或客户]重新构建此消息。消息最初是为[上下文]撰写的。调整语气、措辞和风格以适应预期受众。文本：[粘贴文本]。”

起草会议邀请

Draft meeting invite

“Draft a meeting invitation for a session about [topic]. The meeting will include [attendees/roles] and should outline agenda items, goals, and preparation required. Provide the text in calendar-invite format.”

“为关于[主题]的会议起草邀请函。会议将包括[与会者/角色]，应概述议程项目、目标以及所需的准备。提供日历邀请格式的文本。”

总结长邮件

Summarize long email

“Summarize this email thread into a short recap. The thread includes several back-and-forth messages. Highlight key decisions, action items, and open questions. Email: [paste text].”

“将此邮件线程总结为简短的回顾。线程包括几个来回消息。突出关键决策、行动项和开放性问题。邮件：[粘贴文本]。”

12.2 会议与协作 (Meetings & Collaboration)

创建会议议程

Create a meeting agenda

“Create a structured agenda for a meeting about [topic]. The meeting will last [time] and include [attendees]. Break the agenda into sections with time estimates and goals for each section.”

“为关于[主题]的会议创建结构化议程。会议将持续[时间]，包括[与会者]。将议程分成部分，为每个部分提供时间估计和目标。”

总结会议笔记

Summarize meeting notes

“Summarize these meeting notes into a structured recap. The notes are rough and informal. Organize them into categories: key decisions, next steps, and responsibilities. Notes: [paste text].”

“将这些会议笔记总结为结构化的回顾。笔记是粗略和非正式的。将它们组织成类别：关键决策、下一步行动和责任。笔记：[粘贴文本]。”

创建行动项列表

Create an action items list

“Turn the following meeting notes into a clean task list. The tasks should be grouped by owner and include deadlines if mentioned. Notes: [paste text].”

“将以下会议笔记转换为干净的任务列表。任务应按所有者分组，如果提到的话包括截止日期。笔记：[粘贴文本]。”

为会议准备问题

Prep questions for a meeting

“Suggest thoughtful questions to ask in a meeting about [topic]. The purpose of the meeting is [purpose]. Provide a list of at least 5 questions that show preparation and insight.”

“建议在关于[主题]的会议中提出的有深度的提问。会议的目的是[目的]。提供至少5个显示准备和洞察力的问题的列表。”

起草跟进邮件

Draft follow-up email

“Write a professional follow-up email after a meeting about [topic]. Include a recap of key points, assigned responsibilities, and next steps with deadlines. Use a clear and polite tone.”

“在关于[主题]的会议后撰写专业跟进邮件。包括关键点的回顾、分配的责任，以及带有截止日期的下一步行动。使用清晰和礼貌的语气。”

12.3 问题解决与决策 (Problem Solving & Decision Making)

识别根本原因

Identify root cause

“Analyze the following workplace issue: [describe issue]. The context is that the problem has occurred multiple times. Identify possible root causes and suggest questions to confirm them.”

“分析以下工作场所问题：[描述问题]。背景是这个问题多次发生。识别可能的根本原因并建议确认它们的问题。”

比较选项

Compare options

“Compare the following two or more possible solutions: [list options]. The decision needs to be made in [timeframe]. Evaluate pros, cons, and potential risks for each option.”

“比较以下两个或更多可能的解决方案：[列出选项]。需要在[时间框架]内做出决定。评估每个选项的优缺点和潜在风险。”

决策标准

Decision criteria

“Help define clear decision-making criteria for [describe decision]. The context is that multiple stakeholders are involved. Provide a short list of weighted criteria to guide the choice.”

“帮助为[描述决策]定义清晰的决策标准。背景是多个利益相关者参与其中。提供指导选择的简短加权标准列表。”

风险评估

Risk assessment

“Assess the potential risks of the following plan: [describe plan]. The plan is set to start on [date]. List risks by likelihood and impact, and suggest mitigation strategies.”

“评估以下计划的潜在风险：[描述计划]。计划将于[日期]开始。按照可能性和影响列出风险，并建议缓解策略。”

推荐最佳选项

Recommend best option

“Based on the following background: [describe situation and options], recommend the most suitable option. Explain your reasoning clearly and suggest first steps for implementation.”

“基于以下背景：[描述情况和选项]，推荐最合适的选项。清晰解释你的推理，并建议实施的第一步。”

12.4 组织与生产力 (Organization & Productivity)

记录每日优先事项

Document daily priorities

“Create a prioritized to-do list from the following tasks: [paste tasks]. The context is a typical workday with limited time. Suggest which tasks should be done first and why.”

“从以下任务创建优先级待办事项列表：[粘贴任务]。背景是有限时间的典型工作日。建议哪些任务应该先做以及为什么。”

创建每周计划

Create a weekly plan

“Build a weekly work plan for [describe role or situation]. The week includes deadlines, meetings, and individual focus time. Provide a balanced schedule with recommended priorities.”

“为[描述角色或情况]构建每周工作计划。本周包括截止日期、会议和个人专注时间。提供带有推荐优先级的平衡时间表。”

总结长文档

Summarize a long document

“Summarize the following document into 5 key points and 3 recommended actions. The document is [type: report, plan, or notes]. Keep the summary concise and professional. Text: [paste document].”

“将以下文档总结为5个关键点和3个推荐行动。文档是[类型：报告、计划或笔记]。保持总结简洁且专业。文本：[粘贴文档]。”

头脑风暴解决方案

Brainstorm solutions

“Brainstorm potential solutions to the following workplace challenge: [describe challenge]. Provide at least 5 varied ideas, noting pros and cons for each.”

“为以下工作场所挑战头脑风暴潜在解决方案：[描述挑战]。提供至少5个不同的想法，为每个想法注明优缺点。”

撰写项目更新

Write a project update

“Draft a short project update for stakeholders. The project is [describe project]. Include progress made, current blockers, and next steps. Write in a professional, concise style.”

“为利益相关者起草简短的项目更新。项目是[描述项目]。包括已取得的进展、当前障碍和下一步行动。以专业、简洁的风格撰写。”
